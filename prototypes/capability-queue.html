Microsoft Graph integration with retry & OAuth handling

Walk the capabilities, decide on each concern in turn. Clean capabilities are skipped automatically. 19 noise findings filtered.

What

Adds a typed IGraphService with DI registration, Polly retry pipeline, and a token-caching GraphOAuthMessageHandler. Retires the legacy plugin-based Graph integration.

Why

Per specs/msgraph-di-consolidation.md: the legacy plugin pattern duplicated auth and retry across each Graph caller, so token expiry and rate limiting were handled inconsistently.

How

Standard layered shape — Infra owns the cross-cutting handlers, Services hold the business-facing wrappers, Models hold DTOs. Risk concentrates inside the auth/retry interaction.
