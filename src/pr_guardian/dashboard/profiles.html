How it works

  • Risk score (0–100) is the combined risk of a PR. Low scores can auto-approve; high scores hard-block.
  • Auto-approve max / Hard-block are the two thresholds on that score — between them, a PR goes to human review.
  • Repo risk class shifts how cautious those thresholds are for this repo.
  • Readiness controls when a PR is ripe to review — quiet period, CI waits, and the Archmap artifact.
  • Side effects are the actions Guardian may take on the platform (comments, labels, formal approvals, clearance status).
